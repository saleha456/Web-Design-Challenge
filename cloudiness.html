Cloudiness

Cloudiness appears in all parts of the world regardless of latitude. However, there is less cloudiness near the north and south pole. Perhaps this is because cooler climates hold less moisture in the air or perhaps it's because there aren't any cities located that far north or south from this study. The northern hemisphere seems to have slightly more cloudiness.
